Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Velvet Chapter. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information when you use our services, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, shipping address, and payment details when making a purchase.
  • Browsing Information: IP address, device type, and cookies for website functionality.
  • Communication Data: When you contact us for support or sign up for our newsletter.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Processing orders and delivering products.
  • Improving website experience and customer service.
  • Sending promotional offers (if opted-in).
  • Complying with legal and security requirements.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping companies, and website hosting services.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law to comply with regulations.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure payment gateways.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we store.
  • Correct or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
